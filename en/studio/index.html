Estúdio

we are boq is a design studio created by Guilherme Cartaxo and Miguel Duarte in 2004 and formally established as a company in 2009. We work in the fields of print, web and identity design, with public institutions, international organizations and small companies, mostly in the culture and education sectors. We believe in design as a participative process that cannot be isolated from its context and thus try to actively engage clients and users throughout the whole process.

Miguel Duarte

Since graduating from university in 2000, Miguel has explored the various fields of design including industrial, product, interior, graphic and web design. The experience gained in the time he travelled, lived and worked in Asia, allowed him to learn and explore different cultures, strengthening his sensitivity to context and the way of thinking the design process.
