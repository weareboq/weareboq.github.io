Lula Pena Box

Type Print Year 2011 Client Conserveira de Lisboa

Lula Pena in Ink with Melody is a product from Conserveira de Lisboa containing a music box with a tune composed by the singer Lula Pena and a can of Squid in Ink. To house these two items, we created and designed a cardboard box, based on the visual identity of Conserveira.
