Next Future

Type Web Year 2012 Client Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation

visit

Next Future is a Gulbenkian programme for Contemporary Culture that focuses on research and artistic creations in Europe, Latin America and the Caribbean, and in Africa. In this context, we were invited by the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation to redesign the Next Future website, for which we created a new graphic design and user experience with a responsive layout that adapts to different devices and screen resolutions. We also implemented a new bilingual content management system, migrating all the existing content, and integrating the blog previously hosted in another platform.
