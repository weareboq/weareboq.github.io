Virtual Corvo

Type Web Year 2012 Client SPEA

visit

The Corvo Island Virtual Interpretation Center was created to inform the public and raise awareness about the study and preservation of the environment of this island in the Azores.

The website that we developed with the Portuguese Society for the Study of Birds allows the visitor to perform a virtual visit, with the help of maps and interactive panoramas, to the different cultural and environmental points of interest in the Corvo Island.
