Sindika Dokolo Foundation

Type Web Year 2012 Client Sindika Dokolo Foundation

visit

Sindika Dokolo Foundation is a key institution in the cultural and artistic landscape of the african continent that organizes the Luanda Triennial and holds the most important collection of contemporary african art.

The starting idea for the new website was the use of full screen imagery as the way of showing their work. To accomplish this we created a navigation system halfway between a slideshow and an ebook that presents each project or section as a sequential narrative.
